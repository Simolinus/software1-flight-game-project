Many years ago, a mysterious image was created to represent the collective knowledge, history, and cultural diversity of human civilization. 
This became known as the ‘Map of Knowledge’.

However, due to unknown circumstances, the image was eventually broken into ten separate puzzle pieces. 
These pieces were then scattered across various airports around the world. 
Each puzzle piece was connected to a clue that hinted at the possible location of the next piece.

Many years later, a mysterious document was discovered during excavation in Helsinki. 
The document contained a message that read: 
“Only the one who travels across the world and uncovers all the puzzle pieces, will be able to restore the Map of Knowledge.”

Many people didn’t take it seriously and were afraid to take on this challenge.

Inspired by the difficulty, a new explorer decided to take it on.

Game rules:
Objective: Collect 10 puzzle pieces.
Earn score by completing the game fast, answering quizzes and collecting puzzle pieces.
Earn money by answering quizzes.
